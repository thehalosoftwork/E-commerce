Our Philosophy

Crafted with
Intention

At SOLÈNE, we believe that extraordinary footwear is the intersection of centuries-old craft and contemporary vision. Founded in Florence in 2012, our atelier works with the finest leathers sourced from family-owned tanneries that share our commitment to quality and sustainability.

Every sole is stitched by hand. Every upper shaped on wooden lasts carved to perfection. This is not mass production — it is devotion made tangible.
